By Nandhini Krishnamoorthy

Last Updated 09 September 2021

What is CWA?

Chennai Weekend Artist – is my most desirable community till now. CWA is a group of Artist and Art lovers who gather somewhere in Chennai for painting, “Sorry not only for painting but also for sharing love, kindness and experience”. Art lovers from other countries are also joining virtually. So for me CWA is a Classic World Artist group. One thing I admired in this 15000+ community is – there are art lovers from age 6 to experienced artist age 70 and the talent and maturity the budding artists have is really amazing and we should learn more from them. I can still remember there was one autism child whose age was 14, who can’t able to understand the world of people but he understands and loves the world of colors and he is the best in that.
